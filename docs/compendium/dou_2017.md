      5. Mechanistic spine: cellular_senescence → LMNB1 loss → CCF formation → CGAS recruitment and cGAMP production → STING1 activation → RELA phosphorylation and nuclear translocation → SASP gene expression and cytokine secretion → immune cell recruitment and immunosurveillance.

(d) Specificity: IFI16 and MAVS do not produce the same dependence, distinguishing the cytosolic DNA branch.
